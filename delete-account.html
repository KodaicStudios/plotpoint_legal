What gets deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your profile and personal information
  • Your reading history and progress
  • Your club memberships and messages
  • All uploaded content (books, manga)
  • Your subscription (if active, cancel it first via the store)

⚠️ This action is permanent and cannot be undone. Your data cannot be recovered after deletion.

Request account deletion

Enter the email address associated with your Plot Point account. We'll process your deletion request within 48 hours.

This is optional and only used to improve our service.

You can also delete your account directly in the app:
Settings → Account → Delete Account
